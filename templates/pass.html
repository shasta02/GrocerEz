Get Started

GrocerEz reduces the number of grocery stores you have to visit!

Our application will search grocery stores in your zip code for the list of groceries you are looking for.

Step 1

Enter the groceries you are looking for and your zip code

Step 2

After finalizing your list and zip code, click the submit button.

Step 3

Our Python based backend will search grocery stores near your zip code and determine which products are in stock at each store.
